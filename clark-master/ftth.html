Project Description

This project is enrolled as part of my final year project in Management Information Systems, I got an internship at OOREDOO. internship in the company OOREDOO Tunisia.
It is a solution for managing the FTTH deployment process is envisaged to improve the accessibility and transparency of information, optimize the processing time of FTTH projects and offer a fast and high quality customer service.
It consists in designing and developing a web application for FTTH zone management allowing to manage the horizontal part from the optical connection node (NRO) to the optical sub-distributor (SRO) up to the building, to manage the vertical part or the connection of the customers either from a monosite through the optical connection point (PBO) or from an apartment through the building connection point (PRI) and the floor box (BE)
The other aspect of this project is to provide graphs to FTTH agents to track the progress of projects and the progress of the projects and to know the eligibility of the households to the optical fiber.

In the building of this platform i used SCRUM as work methodology,Angular for the Front-End part,Laravel 5.8 framework for the Back-End part and Github for project management
