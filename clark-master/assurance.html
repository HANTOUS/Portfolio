Provisioning Temporary Life Annuity

Home Portfolio Provisioning Temporary Life Annuity

Project Description

In this project we have calculated the probable value of the life insurance contract for an Italian subject born in 1955 -1959. For this purpose we have made a study and analysis of the mortality rate among Italians in a first part.
Then the implementation of the Lee Carter model for the elaboration of the predictions of mortality rate for the future years.
Finally, we constructed the mortality tables for both sexes in order to calculate the probable present value of the contract for the cohorts born in 1955 (1959) contracted in 2017 -2037 (2021-2041)

I took part of a team of 5 in the build of this platform, we used R for the modeling and calculation,RShiny to display the results in an interface
