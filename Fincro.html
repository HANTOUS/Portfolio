Project Description

This project is carried out as end of year project, it's a digitalized microfinance agency that aims to reach the largest possible number of people at a lower cost and in a very convenient way by giving them the absolute ability to open their own accounts only by using their smartphones, exchange financial informations and transactions in total security and to get permanently updated and constantly informed by the newest offers and market advices.

I took part of a team of 5 in the build of this platform, we used the Unified Process as a work methodology and we used SpringBoot for the Backend part and Angular for the Frontend part consuming RESTful api to communicate between them.

What i did

I was responsible of building the Micro Credit management Module :

  • I used several mathematical formulas for the calculation of the credit interest rate switch a rating system for the client.
  • I created a credit simulateur which the client can use it before choosing to apply for the credit.
  • I created an amortization table so that the client can follow up his repayment schedule.
  • I devoloped other functionalities such as automatic payement rescheduling in case of the client has the capacity to pay more
